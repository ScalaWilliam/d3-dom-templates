William's client-side DOM Template Animation extensions for D3.JS

This is a technical demo. It will make no sense unless you're familiar with D3 and constancy.

In constancy, elements are supposed to be re-used rather than rewritten. This demo shows how constancy is possible in a client-side templating scenario.

Different methods could be applied, depending on your specific application.
